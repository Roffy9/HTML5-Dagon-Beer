International Awards

Prize of the July

Since 2006 till today, at the Monde (World) Selection held by International Institute for Quality Selections,based in Brussels, BELGIUM, products of DAGON Beverages continue to achieve high international quality standing winning numerous awards in Grand Gold and Gold.For its commitment in quality,its products were also awarded “High International Quality Trophies” for achieving high quality level,the “ Crystal Prestige Trophy” (2014) for participation in Monde Selections for 10 consecutive years and the “Prize of the Jury” for DAGON Extra strong Beer (2016) as the world’s best chosen by all the Judges of Monde Selection.

Auatrialina Inaternational Beer Awards(AIBA)

Testimony to international quality standing status, beer products of DAGON Beverages won “ Best European Style Lager” trophy (2017) along with total of (5) Gold Awards, (11) Silver Awards and (16) Bronze Awards since 2008 at the Australian International Beer Awards (AIBA) in Melbourne,AUSTRALIA. The most surprising result is that DAGON Beverages Co., Ltd. has been recognized its supreme prestige as “The Champion Large International Brewery” in 2017 from AIBA.

International Brewing Awards(IBA)

DAGON Beverages marked yet another historical milestone at the International Brewing Awards in ENGLAND, the pinnacle of Brewing Achievement, where DAGON Extra strong won a GOLD Medal Award (2017) and DAGON Super won two SILVER Medal Awards (2015 and 2017).
